Use Comments in Your Terminal

You probably know that you can use comments in bash scripts by starting a line with #. It also works for the terminal!

I like to use it to leave myself super short notes that I can refer back to if I'm doing something involved in the terminal. (For longer notes I like to use Vimwiki.) This also gives you a fast way to search your recent notes by scrolling through your shell's history.
